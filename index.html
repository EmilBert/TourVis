TourVis

The default heatmap shows which countries people worldwide travel to. A more intense color means more travellers. Selecting a region from the buttons shows where people from that specific region travel to in the world. Selecting a country shows a barplot that specifies the origin of the travellers to that selected country. The slider at the bottom is used to change the year, which updates the visualization








*Note all values are given in thousands, i.e 100 => 100,000
